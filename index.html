ABOUT US

Founded in 2012, InterProducTec has access to research, resources, experience and know-how through the founder’s family business, the Precitec Group.

Today, InterProducTec has presence in New York, Munich and Mumbai.

The ambitious young team consists of about 50 hardware and software engineers, who dedicate their passion and work to provide new and cutting edge technologies to the world of smart products and automation.

Interproductec is an AI company of innovation. We’re proud to play a critical role in helping businesses to solve their most complex processes with Artificial Intelligence
